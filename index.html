About Century Gym

Established in 2015, **Century Gym ** is Chandragadi premier fitness destination for serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts. Our state-of-the-art facility spans 15,000 sq ft with cutting-edge equipment imported from Germany and USA.

We specialize in **powerlifting**, **Olympic weightlifting**, **functional training**, and **personalized coaching**. Over 5,000 members have transformed their bodies with our proven training methodologies.
